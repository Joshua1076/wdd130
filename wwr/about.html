Rapid Rush Rafting

At Rapid Rush Rafting, our mission is to create unforgettable white-water adventures that connect people with nature, teamwork, and pure excitement on the river.

History

Founded in 2008, Rapid Rush Rafting began as a small family business with a big dream — to share the thrill of white-water rafting with adventurers from around the world. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted and experienced rafting companies.
